Hey, I'm Hang Vo
When I was small, I'd always had a dream of having a house of my own and having my personal style imprinted in it. Growing up, I chose to become an interior designer to fulfill my dream as well as to help make other people's dream house come true.
.
I have 6 years of experience working in interior. With each project I received, I'd like to get my hand in full-stack concept from choosing the style, colors, materials; sketching my idea; to selecting material samples so that the work is completed in accordance with the most original idea. As an interior designer, I require myself to be a good listener and disciplined to be able to handle work flexibly and meet the deadline.
.
Off work, I like to travel on my own and explore the world around me, discover new things, enjoy delicious food, connect and meet new friends and enjoy the best time of my life.
